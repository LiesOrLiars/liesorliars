The Record

Every case in these archives is built on primary documents. Court transcripts. Affidavits. Evidence logs. Police reports obtained through public record requests. No paraphrasing. No second-hand accounts. The document speaks for itself.

The Method

Each file begins with what was officially recorded and moves toward what the record reveals on close reading. Inconsistencies in sworn testimony. Omissions in chain-of-custody logs. The gap between what was said under oath and what was physically documented.

The Standard

No speculation without documentation. No conclusions without citations. If it cannot be sourced to a filed record, it does not appear here. This is not a podcast. It is not an opinion. It is an archive.
